# From Expert to Master

Welcome to the Experts guide! In this guide you will learn new Flint features regarding concurrency and multithreaded processing in general. After these initial language feature chapters will follow a few chapters describing Flint internals in-depth. After this chapter, you will exactly know what happens at runtime in any Flint program.

The goal of this guide is to teach how Flint works under the hood, and to complete its transparency promise by showing how everything maps to lower levels.

So, let's get started!
